Admission Procedure

1. First Year Degree College Online Admission Procedure 2023-2024
Fill the University of Mumbai Pre-Registration Form 2023-2024 by visiting the given website: http://mum.digitaluniversity.ac/ Select MSG-SGKM College of Arts, Science and Commerce and college code 930. Please keep the scanned copies of your HSC Marksheet, HSC Leaving Certificate & SSC Passing Certificate ready before you connect to the http://mum.digitaluniversity.ac/ as you are required to upload the same while submitting the form on http://mum.digitaluniversity.ac/. Take the hard copy/ soft copy of the pre-registration form 2023-2024 and keep it ready before you proceed to Step 2.

3. On successful uploading/submission of the pre-admission form of the college along with the documents, the application will be verified by the admission department of the college.

4. Incomplete admission form or forms with incorrect information will be rejected.

5. All verified and approved forms shall be considered for selection in the merit list. The merit list shall be displayed on the college notice board and website www.ghatkopar.universalcollege.edu.in

6. On selection in the Merit List, students shall receive an SMS and an email regarding the admission process, fee details and the payment procedure.

Admissions will be confirmed after payment of fees and physical verification of original documents within the stipulated time. If the fee is not paid by the applicant within the stipulated time period, he/she will lose the claim of admission.
